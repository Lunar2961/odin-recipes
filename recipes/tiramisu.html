Tiramisu Recipe

Classic Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is fire as fuck. If you haven't tried it, do so and thank me later

Ingredients

  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup mascarpone
  • 1 3/4 cups good ass espresso
  • 2 tablespoons rum or cognac
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • A buncha ladyfingers
  • 2 ounces dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Whip egg yolks and quarter cup sugar, then put into larger mixing bowl
  2. Whip cream and rest of sugar then add mascarpone and continue to whip until soft
  3. Gently fold mascarpone mixture into sweetened egg yolks
  4. Combine cognac and espresso into a shallow bowl and set aside for now
  5. Have a nice glass of cognac as you continue this recipe -- it'll be a minute
  6. Sift one tablespoon cocoa powder over the bottom of 8x8" baking dish
  7. Quickly dip ladyfinger into rum and espresso mixture & put in dish round-side up. Do this with half the ladyfingers, making an even layer
  8. Spread half the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers
  9. Repeat steps 5 and 7
  10. Top with the remaining mascarpone mixture, then sift the remaining cocoa powder. Top with shaved dark chocolate
  11. Cover with plastic wrap and let chill for at least 4 hours, though an entire day is preferred
  12. Finish bottle of cognac
  13. Eat tiramisu shamlessly in your underwear in the middle of the night in a drunken stupor
